About us

About us

Global Bridge Shipping Ltd. was founded in 2015 as a Freight Forward approved by Bangladesh government. We GBSL keeps steady growing with 2 branches in Bangladesh which is Dhaka and Chittagong. We GBSL is a one stop forwarding solutions for you.

We have been working to promote the rapid and all-round development of the logistics service industry in Bangladesh over the years. We have Worldwide efficient service networks can cover all major ports globally. Global Bridge Shipping Ltd. provide you services for simple fright movements for Sea, Air and road, managing complex supply chain, LCL consolidation, warehouse service, custom clearance and integrated.

Services

SEA FREIGHT


GLOBAL BRIDGE SHIPPING can offer all different levels of Sea freight transport. Our sea freight expertise ensure your cargo is handled on the most competitive route with the most reliable carrier. We have good agreement with a variety of consolidators and shipping lines to provide regular sailings, flexible transit times and competitive rates. Our exclusive contracts allow for top-tier rates which provide substantial cost savings. Whether your shipment is urgent or non-pressing, we can offer many different transit time options. However, timeliness is never compromised. A comprehensive range of our Sea Freight Services include:

  • All kind of Export / Import Consolidation
  • Handling FCL & LCL shipments to world wide destinations
  • Port-Port & Door-Door service
  • Sea- Air shipment
  • Flexible Pickup Timing
  • All Necessary Customs Clearance
  • Quality Control – Inspection & PO management
